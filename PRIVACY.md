# AgenticFlowX Privacy Policy

## Last Updated: May 19, 2026

AgenticFlowX respects your privacy and is committed to transparency about how we handle your data.

## Telemetry

AgenticFlowX includes **optional, opt-in** anonymous usage analytics via [Microsoft Clarity](https://clarity.microsoft.com/). It is enabled by default and can be toggled in **Settings → Privacy → Allow anonymous error and usage reporting**.

When enabled, Clarity collects:

- Session replays (UI layout, clicks, scrolls, navigation)
- Aggregated heatmaps and UI interaction metrics
- JavaScript error stack traces from the extension UI

When disabled, no Clarity script is loaded and no data is sent.

## Your Data

- **Code & files**: Accessed locally only. Relevant context is sent to your chosen AI provider (e.g., Anthropic, OpenAI) to fulfill requests — never to AgenticFlowX.
- **API keys**: Stored locally on your device and sent only to the provider you select. Never sent to AgenticFlowX.
- **Commands**: Execute in your local environment. Context may be forwarded to your AI provider as part of a request.

## What We Don't Do

- No cloud backend or proxy — AI calls go directly from your machine to your provider.
- No collection of your code, prompts, or AI conversations.
- No selling or sharing of your data.
- No model training on your data.

## Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue at <https://github.com/AgenticFlowX/agenticflowx/issues>.
